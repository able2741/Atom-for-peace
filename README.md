## 📖 About
Many people fear nuclear technology because of myths and
misunderstandings. Peaceful Atom was created to change that
— replacing fear with facts, one section at a time.

The app covers:
- 🏥 Medicine — cancer treatment, medical imaging
- ⚡ Energy — clean nuclear power
- 🌱 Agriculture — food safety, crop improvement
- 🏭 Industry — radiography, safety inspection
- 🌳 Environment — pollution tracking, wildlife protection
- 📜 History — Atoms for Peace and beyond
- 🛡️ Safety — ALARA, radiation protection principles
- 💭 Misconceptions vs Reality
- ❓ FAQ
- 📖 Glossary of key nuclear terms
- 🧠 Myth or Fact Quiz
- 😂 Fun Zone Quiz
- 🏅 Certificate of completion
